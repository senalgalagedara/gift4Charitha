Kuweni Concert

The Cinematic Musical Tour

‘Kuweni’ is a series of concerts, expanded over 5 years of musical and visual story-telling, curated from original compositions drawing inspiration from Sri Lankan, Indian and Western music, together with academically and professionally acclaimed musicians, forming novel multiple year-on-year cinematic musical concerts for a dedicated audience.

Curated by the internationally acclaimed Visual Arts and Experiences Group (VAEG), the show uses strong visuals and lighting techniques to narrate a cinematic story telling experience. VAEG uses state-of- the- art equipment to map out and construct this groundbreaking visual experience, which synergises alongside the musical compositions, thereby giving the audience a full cinematic musical experience.
